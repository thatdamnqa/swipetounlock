Swipe to Unlock

Swipe To Unlock is a website for tips about computers away from the desk.

We all carry mobile phones in our pockets and many of us have iPads on our sofa, which are just as powerful as the laptops on our desk. And being within arms reach at all times, these devices are often more useful and convenient.

But are we using them the best we can? There’s so many hidden features which you probably didn’t know about, better ways to do things, and a world of apps out there.

My mission is to share as much as I can, and who knows: you may find something to make your device using life that much easier!
